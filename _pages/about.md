Hi, I'm **Guixian Zhang**, currently a PhD candidate in Artificial Intelligence (Computer Science and Technology) at China University of Mining and Technology, supervised by Prof. [Guan Yuan](http://faculty.cumt.edu.cn/yuanguan/zh_CN/index.htm). Before that, I was pursuing my master's degree at Guangxi Normal University under the supervision of Prof. [Shichao Zhang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=c8G6JzIAAAAJ&hl=en). Meanwhile, I'm extra focused on AI applications in education, social networks, physics and biomedicine. I am also hobbyist in photography, cookery and football.
